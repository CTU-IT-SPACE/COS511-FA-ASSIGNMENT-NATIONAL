# COS511 FA1 Video Submissions

> **Important:** This repository is **only for submitting your FA1 video links and summaries**.  
> Do **not** upload any video files or other documents here.  
>  
> To submit your video:
> 1. Record your FA1 video (≤10 minutes).  
> 2. Upload it to **YouTube as Unlisted** and copy the link.  
> 3. Go to the **Issues tab** and create a new issue using the **FA1 Submission template**.  
> 4. Paste your YouTube link and fill in your summary.  
>  
> This keeps all video submissions centralized and easy to review.
